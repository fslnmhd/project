ABOUT US

The NFGTA Collage Of Engineering, founded in 1921, is Sri lanka’s oldest Higher Education Institution. In keeping with its motto(Buddhi Sarvathra Bhrajate), translated from Sanskrit as “Wisdom Enlightens”, the NFGTA Collage Of Engineering continually strives to maintain academic excellence in all fields of study. The Collage is organized across 4 Faculties and 59 academic departments, along with 7 Institutes, a Collage, a School, and 20+ Centres & Units. Located primarily in the Colombo metropolitan region, the NFGTA Collage Of Engineering is structured as a state institution which provides access to higher education across the country.The main campus is sited in the heart of Colombo, on a sprawling 50-acre estate, and comprises contemporary urban constructions as well as old colonial architecture considered national heritage sites.

The Collage is a proud member of the Asian Collages Alliance of which the Executive Presidency for the period 2019-2020 is held by the NFGTA Collage Of Engineering. In addition to a large and diverse academic portfolio, the NFGTA Collage Of Engineering also offers library services, career guidance, sports,student societies and accommodation on Collage. Other facilities include an arts theatre where students can showcase their skills in performing arts.

The NFGTA Collage Of Engineering actively engages in improving student life in numerous ways to supplement academic learning. Over the years, the Collage has built strong links with the industry in order to enhance the value of the education we provide. Similarly, the sports facilities provided by the Collage encourage the students to balance academics and social life, and many national and international achievements are recorded by our students every year in the sports arena.
